Who are we?

By cyclists and for cyclists, Rent a Ride launched in 2024 with a mission: to invite you into the beautiful world of road, MTB or track cycling without the need to buy a bike.

Pigeon is our CEO. She is responsible for selecting the bikes on our catalog, and also organizes some of the group rides.

Our bike mechanic, Pug, is an expert on fine-tunning each bike, so you can be sure to be riding quality at every pedal stroke.

Mandarin performs a basic bike fit each time you Rent-A-Ride, but he can also perform a full bike fit on your own machine if you wish. He is the one driving the van during our group rides.

You can find us at Evergreen St. 742, Mexico City, call us a 55-0000-0000, or write us to conctact@rentaride.com
